# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Be kind.** These exercises are designed for beginners. There are no stupid questions.

**Be constructive.** If something is wrong, explain what and why. If you can, suggest a fix.

**Be patient.** Not everyone has the same background. What's obvious to you might be new to someone else.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing someone for their experience level
- Spam or off-topic self-promotion

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [hello@zerothstudios.com](mailto:hello@zerothstudios.com). All complaints will be reviewed and addressed.
